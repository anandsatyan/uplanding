What we do


Upaghna builds great companies.
We are a venture capital fund, but with some key differences:

We are investors and co-founders. We fund and create promising companies.

Our companies start within Upaghna:
  • with an amazing idea, or
  • with an amazing co-founder.

We ideate, research, prototype, validate, and fund. We provide top-tier core services. Our companies have full Upaghna support: engineering, marketing, operations, accounting, design, finance, HR, IT, and recruiting.

Our co-founders focus on building the business. Upaghna experts handle the rest.

What we don't do


  • We don’t take pitches (VC)
  • We don’t take applications (accelerator)
  • We don’t move slowly
